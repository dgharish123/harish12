Kerala Tour Packages

Kerala, a state on India's tropical Malabar Coast, has nearly 600km of Arabian Sea shoreline. It's known for its palm-lined beaches and backwaters, a network of canals. National parks like Eravikulam and Periyar, plus Wayanad and other sanctuaries, are home to elephants, langur monkeys and tigers.

About Kerala Tour Packages

KERALA also known as the “God’s own country” is one of the most beautiful states of the Southern India with a long and beautiful coastline. Kerala has something extraordinary to offer to every type of traveller and is termed as one of the ten paradises on earth by the National Geographic Traveller. Kerala is known for its mix of Indian and Dravidian culture, palm lined beaches, sloppy mountains,rejuvenating Ayurveda , exquisite tea and coffee plantations, fragrant spices, distinctive backwaters, green shredded coconut trees, melodious music and vibrant handicrafts. Eravikulam and periyar national parks, Munnar and Wyannad hills,Varkala and Kovalam beach, Alleppey backwaters are a must visit for a traveler visiting Kerala.
